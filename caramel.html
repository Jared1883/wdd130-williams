Salted Caramel Ice Cream

Ingredients

Ice Cream Base:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp sea salt (adjust to taste)

Optional Add-ins:

  • Caramel swirls, toffee bits, or chopped pecans

Instructions:

1. Make the caramel:

  • In a saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar and water. Stir until dissolved, then stop stirring and let it cook until it turns amber.

2. Add butter and cream:

  • Reduce heat to low. Carefully whisk in butter, then slowly whisk in heavy cream (it will bubble up).

3. Add milk, vanilla, and salt:

  • Stir in milk, vanilla extract, and sea salt until smooth.

4. Chill:

  • Cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for 2–4 hours until fully chilled.

5. Churn the ice cream:

  • Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.

6. Freeze:

  • Transfer to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

7. Serve:

  • Scoop and enjoy — add a caramel drizzle or toffee bits if you want extra crunch.
